# Working Backwards: The PR/FAQ

Amazon's product development process famously starts with a **Press Release (PR)** and **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)** document before writing a single line of code.

## The Press Release
The PR should clearly articulate:
1. **The Customer Problem:** What is the painful problem the customer is facing today?
2. **The Solution:** How does your new product elegantly solve this problem?
3. **The Customer Experience:** What does the customer feel when they use it?

*Keep the PR to exactly one page.* If you can't explain the value in one page, the product is too complex.

## The Internal FAQ
This is where the product manager gets grilled. Anticipate the hardest questions stakeholders will ask:
- How much will this cost to build?
- Why will customers switch from competitors?
- What are the privacy implications?

## The External FAQ
Questions the actual customers will ask:
- How much does it cost?
- How do I cancel?
- Does it integrate with my existing tools?
